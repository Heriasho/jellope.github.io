Guild News

Whitemane Queues: In recent weeks the server queue has mellowed out, mostly appearing as a 'High' level server and with only up to a 1000 person queue at its high times.

Quest to 60: The race is on! In a few weeks time a majority of the guild will be 60 and events will begin to be scheduled. One thing the officers would like to do is encourage a friendly competition to reach max level, with details to follow in the future.

Recruitment: As we near max level the officers feel that we will need to flesh out our ranks to prepare for late game raids and dungeon. In particular, we are interested in recruiting a series of tanks and healers to bolster our guild. We actively encourage everyone to be on the look out for skilled individuals who would be interested in joining our guild.
