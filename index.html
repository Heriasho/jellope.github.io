Shell Museum Website

Platforms: Windows

I took up the role as a project lead of a team as well as web designer for the Shell Marinez Alumni Museum and assembled a website template from the ground up along aside a server to house it. As part of the process I maintained contact with our clients and assembled a websiting using Bootstrap to form the design of the website, with CSS to add color and style variants along with base html commands. Along side this I helped coordinate the construction of a server based out of a custom built computer on ubuntu linex. In addition, we experiemented with javascript commands to simplify repetitive code, assist in making an ssh key login, and used Winrarr as well as Filezilla to enable our clients to modify the website as they wished.

Personal D&D Website

Platforms: Windows

Ever since I started playing D&D, my friends and I didn't have a way to track our stats outside of logging it on a private Facebook group. To over come this issue, I decided to design a website that would allow my players to have an online resource to view my maps, my npcs, and keep track of their quest objects. In later additions, I made the website mobile friendly and added a mini collection of major D&D spells so that, eventually the player wouldn't need to buy or bring expensive books to their games. I wanted to lower the barrier of entry in game I'm very passionate about.

JavaScript Minesweeper-alike

Platforms: Windows

Origin: JavaScript

In an effort to combine my interests of web design and game development, I worked with a Mindsweeper design pattern and sought to expand its mechanics, all in the browser. The objective of the game is to hunt down certain treasure thieves that are wondering through an obfuscated minefield. After each mouse click, these thieves would move once step towards hidden treasure on the map and disarm bombs along the way, revealing them and reducing score. This prompted the player to plan out whether to wait for disarmed bombs to reveal nearby thieves to catch them or take big risks to sweep the mines to find the theives for higher score.

Bright Bots Factory

Platforms: Windows

Origin: Unity Engine

As part of the Progressive Gamejam, put on by the Sacramento SquareOne club, Bright Bots is a Puzzle solving story game I took the roll of Lead System Designer by designing mockups, building systems, and coordinating the efforts of our programmers. Set in the Cyberpunk setting, using the Unity Engine, You're an unpaid computer hardware intern working for the up and coming Bright Bots Robots factory as their lead designer. You are underappreciated by your boss and your work is scrutanized constantly by the media, but your work can change the world..for better or worse. Players build and design robot CPU's using a variety of shape and color piece to accomidate for a particular task OR intentionally misdesign robots to cause havoic & prevent yourself from being replaced by bots themselves.
